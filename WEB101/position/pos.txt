Relative:
1. Element gets removed from the document flow and its space is reserved.

Absolute:
1. Removes the element completely from the document flow.
2. Will become absolute to parent if parent has position relative, absolute, fixed or sticky.

Fixed:
1. Position is based on entire html element and not on parent.
2. Remain at the same place when you scroll.

Sticky:
1. Combination of fixed and relative.
2. When scrolled outside of page behaves like fixed.
